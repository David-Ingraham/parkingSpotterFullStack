Key Features:

‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

How does Parking Spotter work?

Simply allow location access or search for an NYC address, select how many cameras you want to see (1-8), and view live camera feeds showing current street conditions to find available parking spots.

Where does the camera data come from?

We use public NYC Department of Transportation (DOT) traffic camera data. This app works exclusively in New York City.

Do you store my personal information?

No. We're privacy-focused and don't collect or store any personal data. Location is only used to find nearby cameras.

Is the app free to use?

Currently, Parking Spotter is free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future to support continued development.
